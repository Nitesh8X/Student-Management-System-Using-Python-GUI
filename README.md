<h2>About the College </h2>
<p>
The Nobel Laureate <b>Mother Teresa </b>unveiled the plaque that heralded the beginning of St. Andrews College of Arts , science and commerce on <u>1st January 1981</u>. After gaining permisssion from Government of Maharashtra and the University of Mumbai, the degree college was inaugurated on <u>9th July 1983</u>. This college has come a long way since then and today offer its students good infrastructure, experience faculty, efficient administration and a wide repertoire of courses.
</p>
